Building Analysis & Advisory

Making sense of building performance requires knowledge, experience, insight and a common-sense approach to building systems.

OUT-2 Design provides a range of Building Analysis, and Advisory Services tailored to suit the needs of real estate professionals, developers, investors, and end-users (tenants). Whether you need to benchmark and evaluate multiple buildings against specific performance criteria or undertake technical due diligence and compliance assessments, we have the expertise to support you. Our experience extends beyond our in-depth knowledge of international compliance criteria, to include certification experience using BREEAM, GREEN MARK, GREENSTAR, LEED, and LOTUS.

We apply the Intelligent Buildings Rating Methodology (IBRM) to rapidly measure, benchmark, evaluate and rate the performance of buildings across a variety of functions including offices, schools, research & development, industrial, retail and more.

Outside our knowledge of recognised criteria, our problem-solving mindset and hands-on building experience across Asia enabled us to develop bespoke solutions for complex problems ranging from multi-site phasing strategies to complex maintenance and building upgrade challenges.
