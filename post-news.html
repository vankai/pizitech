Ho Tram Water Safety Community Pool

April 15, 2012

In 2013, The Royal Life Saving Society of Australia release an alarming figure showing that the number of children that drowned in Australia that year had increased to 31.... IN VIETNAM 32 CHILDREN DIE BY DROWNING EVERY DAY.

In a concerted effort to combat these tragic circumstances, Indochine Engineering (ICE) with the support of OUT-2 Design, created the Ho Tram Community Pool; a facility where Water Safety Vietnam have been able to teach almost 1000 children to swim, and over 300 local adult how to teach swimming.
